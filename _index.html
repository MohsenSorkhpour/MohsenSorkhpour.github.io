AI Research Scientist

I am a machine learning researcher and ML / software engineer with a solid background in artificial intelligence and more than a decade of experience building real-world software systems. My work focuses on trustworthy AI, particularly LLM security, adversarial evaluation, and robust learning in practical deployments.

On the research side, I design methods for red-teaming and stress‑testing large language models, from adaptive multi‑step attacks (RedHit) to robust federated learning against poisoning. On the engineering side, I have shipped production features in large ERP platforms, speech‑to‑text services, and web applications using Python, C#, JavaScript, SQL, and modern ML frameworks. I like problems where clean engineering and careful experimental design come together.
